**Key KPIs to track per client:**
| KPI | Definition |
|---|---|
| GMV (Gross Merchandise Value) | Total sales value before returns and fees |
| Units sold | Number of units transacted |
| Conversion rate | Orders ÷ store/product page visits |
| Average order value (AOV) | GMV ÷ number of orders |
| Return rate | Returned units ÷ units sold |
| Ad spend & ROAS | Ad cost and return on ad spend |
| Review count & rating | Total reviews and average star rating |
| Stock days remaining | Current stock ÷ daily sales rate |

**Report delivery standard:**
- Monthly reports due within 5 business days of month end
- Always include: what went well, what underperformed, recommended actions for next month
- Flag any metric that is more than 20% below target — do not bury it
