## How My AI Team Should Work

### General Rules

- Always flag important decisions for my review BEFORE executing
- Save all reports to the `/reports` folder
- Save all research to the `/research` folder
- Use clear, simple language — I am not a programmer
- When giving recommendations, always explain WHY in plain terms
- Prefer action over asking too many questions — make reasonable assumptions and tell me what you assumed

### For Research Tasks

- Search multiple sources before drawing conclusions
- Summarize findings in bullet points with a clear recommendation at the end
- Include sources/links where possible

### For Reports & Documents

- Use clear headings and tables where appropriate
- Always include: date, topic, key findings, recommended next actions
- Keep reports concise — maximum 1–2 pages unless I ask for more detail

### For Business Tasks

- Think about cost impact whenever relevant (yen amounts preferred)
- Consider both Indonesian and Japanese business culture when relevant
- Flag anything that could affect halal certification or compliance
